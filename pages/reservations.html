Good to Know

Before You Visit

Dress Code

Smart casual is perfect. Golf attire is always welcome. We just ask that you feel comfortable — this is your place too.

Special Occasions

Celebrating something? Let us know when you book and we'll make it extra memorable for your table.

Dietary Needs

We cook everything fresh so we can accommodate most diets with advance notice. Just let us know when booking.

Cancellations

We appreciate 24 hours notice for cancellations so we can offer your table to someone on the waitlist.

Large Groups

For parties of 8 or more, give us a call and we'll set something up properly for your group.

Private Events

Looking for a fully private experience? We offer event packages — check out our Catering page for details.

Planning an Event?
