Our Story

Rooted in the
Land Around Us

"Every dish is an expression of where we are — the soil, the seasons, and the spirit of this place."

Pasture + Market was born from a belief that exceptional dining should be deeply local. Perched at the edge of the Lac La Biche Golf & Country Club, our kitchen draws from the region's finest farms and purveyors, transforming the abundance of each season into menus that surprise and satisfy. We believe in unhurried meals, warm service, and food that feels like a celebration.
