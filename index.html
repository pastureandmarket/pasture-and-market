Our Story

Rooted in the
Land Around Us

🍃
"Real food made with real ingredients — that's not a trend, that's just how we cook."

Pasture + Market sits at the edge of the Lac La Biche Golf & Country Club, where the view out the window is as good as what's on the plate. We source everything as close to home as possible — local farms, small producers, and the kind of suppliers who care as much as we do.

Nothing here is frozen, pre-made, or phoned in. Every morning our kitchen team starts fresh — breaking down whole cuts, washing just-arrived greens, cracking real eggs. We believe that's the only way to do it right.
