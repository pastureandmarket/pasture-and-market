Our Story

Rooted in the
Land Around Us

"Every dish is an expression of where we are — the soil, the seasons, and the spirit of this place."

Pasture + Market was born from a belief that exceptional dining should be deeply local. Perched at the edge of the Lac La Biche Golf & Country Club, our kitchen draws from the region's finest farms and purveyors, transforming the abundance of each season into menus that surprise and satisfy. We believe in unhurried meals, warm service, and food that feels like a celebration.

Our Philosophy

The Art of
Honest Cuisine

At Pasture + Market, we believe that great food starts long before it reaches the kitchen. It begins in the soil — in the relationships we've built with Alberta's most dedicated farmers, ranchers, and artisan producers. We bring their work to life on the plate.

Set within the natural splendour of the Lac La Biche Golf & Country Club, our dining room is a place where the landscape inspires the menu, and where every visit feels like a return home. Whether you're here for a quiet morning, a post-round lunch, or a long, indulgent dinner — you belong here.
